About this database

This database indexes replication datasets from peer-reviewed research in Historical Political Economy. Articles are drawn from leading political science and economics journals and classified automatically.

Geographic coding

Countries are coded by geographic continuity: the modern country name is used for the area studied, regardless of which historical polity controlled it at the time. Papers studying the Weimar Republic, Nazi Germany, and the DDR are all coded as Germany. Papers on the British Raj are coded as India. For papers studying a multi-country empire as such, all modern successor states are listed.

Country names follow ISO 3166-1 English short names. Sub-regional groupings follow the UN M.49 standard.
